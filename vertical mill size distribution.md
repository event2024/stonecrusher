The size distribution refers to the range of particle sizes present in the ground material. In industries such as cement or mining, the desired product characteristics, such as fineness or specific surface area, are directly influenced by the particle size distribution. When the feed material contains a narrow particle size range, the mill can operate at a lower energy consumption due to reduced internal circulation and transportation of oversized particles.
